The Terrace

Rocky Point's rooftop hangout

Margaritas Terraza Bar is where Puerto Peñasco comes to unwind. Open air, easy vibe, and a view of the night from up on the terrace. Locals and visitors share the same tables, the same playlist, and the same round of margaritas.

The grill stays busy, the bar pours generous, and the music keeps the night going. Come for the food, stay for the karaoke, and close the place down. We are open late, every night.

Sip & Bite

From the bar, off the grill

Generous pours and honest Sonoran plates. Here is what the terrace is known for.

🍸

House Margaritas

The reason for the name. Classic lime or ask for the flavor of the night, served cold with a salted rim.

🍹

Cocktails

Micheladas, palomas, and the classics, mixed strong and easy to sip on a warm Peñasco night.

🍺

Cold Beer

Buckets of national favorites, ice cold. Perfect with the game on and friends at the table.

🌮

Tacos

Off the grill and made to order. A local favorite that pairs with everything on the bar menu.

🍕

Pizza

Hot, shareable, and ready when the night runs long. Made for a full table.

🦐

Aguachile & Seafood

Fresh from the Sea of Cortez. Bright, spicy, and exactly what you want by the water.

Ask your server about tonight's specials and happy hour.
