The Terrace

Where the night gets good.

We are the rooftop in the middle of Rocky Point, the one with the ceiling packed wall to wall with red, yellow, green, and blue umbrellas. String lights overhead, the town and the night sky all around, and a neon OPEN sign that says the party is on. Pull up a chair with your crew. This is where the night gets good.

Come for one drink, stay til 3 AM. That tends to happen up here. Locals, friends from Arizona, big tables and loud laughs, everyone under the same colorful roof. Grab your photo under the umbrellas while you are at it. Everybody does.

Drinks & Food

Cold drinks, hot food, and a kitchen that keeps going while the music plays. Here is what lands on the table.

House Margaritas

Frozen and on the rocks, the reason our name is on the sign. Lime classic or ask for the flavor you want. Order it by the pitcher and share.

Micheladas

Tall, ice cold, with a thick chili-salt rim. The Rocky Point classic, done right. The first sip explains everything.

Cocktails

Whatever gets the night going. Tell the bar what you are in the mood for and we will pour it.

Cold Beer & Buckets

Beer so cold it hurts, by the bottle or by the bucket on ice. Buckets are made for the table. Get one, then get another.

Tacos

Hot off the grill, just like they should be. Order a round for the table and keep them coming.

Pizza

Yes, pizza on the roof. Perfect for sharing between rounds, and nobody ever says no.

Aguachile

Fresh, citrusy, with a kick. The one that goes hand in hand with a cold beer. Bring napkins.

Seafood & Grill

Fresh seafood and food off the grill, made for sharing with the whole crew. We are on the coast, after all.

Ask your server for today’s prices and specials.

Karaoke

The Game on the Big Screen
